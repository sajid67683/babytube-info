We Create New

'Babytube' has given children and teenagers the opportunity to watch videos online safely. The video sharing app modeled after YouTube was created by two local youths Samim Asraf and Sajjadul Islam.

  • Babytube users will get two benefits. First, just like YouTube, anyone can create and upload videos to the site. Easy terms monetization facility is also available for video creators. As a result, income can also be earned. And users can watch video content for free.
  • Secondly, BabyTube offers premium content. Content creators can earn by uploading their content to BabyTube and users can subscribe to watch videos at a very low cost. Content creators themselves can open accounts and upload their own videos and set their own prices.
  • BabyTube can watch free content as well as premium content.
  • But there is only one condition, the videos must be suitable for children and teenagers.

With Efficiency to More Opportunities

BabyTube has more than 600 videos on various topics including sports, cartoons, education, drama, movies, games, music, travel, blogs, technology. The videos have been viewed more than seven million times. The BabyTube app, which was launched in April last year, has already been downloaded nearly 40,000 times. With 31,000 registered users, 'BabyTube' has enabled children and teenagers to watch videos safely online.
